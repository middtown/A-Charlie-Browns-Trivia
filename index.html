A Charlie Brown's Trivia

How To Play?

  • This is a two player game. But hey, you can play with your invisible friend!
  • Each player has 7 questions. after a total of 14 questions, a winner will be announced.
  • As many rounds as you would like, but keep track of your round per score (kids need the orgainizational practice).
  • Which ever player has the largest score wins!
